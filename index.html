Game Features

RELAXED GAMEPLAY

Engage in a variety of small town activities at your own pace – build friendships, find love, harvest crops, tend livestock, fish, forage, mine, cook, discover magical secrets and restore your dusty old farmhouse.

A DYNAMIC VILLAGE

Explore a dynamic seaside town that grows and changes as seasons pass by. Sugar Blossom is rich in history and culture, and plays host to a variety of festival mini-games and market events throughout the year.

CHARMING CHARACTERS

Befriend a charming cast of 32 individual villagers, all with their own personalities and quirks. Get to know them through a deep relationship system and hundreds upon hundreds of lines of unique situational dialogue.

ROLE PLAYING GAME

Choose your own character and become the most infamous villager in the village.

Hours of GAMEPLAY

More than 30+ hours of pure gameplay!

Engaging Farming Simulation

Grow, plant and harvest your crops after hard working hours. Grow your cattle or livestock by giving them food from which they need to survive.

Game Story

Farm, befriend townsfolk and discover secrets in World's Dawn, a colorful farming adventure!

World's Dawn is a colorful life and social sim set in a sleepy seaside Sugar Blossom village. Soak in the enchanting atmosphere of Sugar Blossom Village as you build friendships, find love, harvest crops, tend livestock, customize your farmhouse, discover magical secrets and more!
